Welcome to the official River Valley Church app. 

Our app lets you stream sermons from Pastor Rob Ketterling, access SOAP devotions, and find out what's going on with River Valley Church in Minnesota. 

River Valley Church exists to lead people to an authentic, life-changing relationship with Jesus Christ. For more information, please visit http://www.rivervalley.org
